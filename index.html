What we Do

Apex Sanctuary provides professional support and care services to individuals in their own homes across PE7, Peterborough, Cambridgeshire and surrounding areas. Our aim is to ensure the well-being of the individuals we care for and that they lead a healthier and happier lifestyle.

We offer person-centred help and support to our service users 24/7, ranging from short-term to long-term care depending on each person's individual needs. Our expert team includes doctors, nurses, social workers, and experienced carers, all dedicated to providing exceptional and safe care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional, person-centred care that empowers individuals to live independently and with dignity in their own homes. We are committed to providing high-quality support services that enhance quality of life and promote well-being.

With our diverse team of qualified professionals including doctors, nurses, social workers, and experienced carers, we ensure good quality services that prioritise your loved ones' needs. Our approach is holistic, considering not just physical needs but also emotional, social, and psychological well-being.
