What we Do

Apex Sanctuary provides professional support and care services to individuals in their own homes across PE7, Peterborough, Cambridgeshire and surrounding areas. Our aim is to ensure the well-being of the individuals we care for and that they lead a healthier and happier lifestyle.

We offer person-centred help and support to our service users 24/7, ranging from short-term to long-term care depending on each person's individual needs. Our expert team includes doctors, nurses, social workers, carers, and support workers, all dedicated to providing exceptional and safe care.

Our comprehensive range of services includes Supported Living, End of Life Care, Home Care, Dementia/Alzheimer's Care, High Dependency Care, and many more specialist services. We work closely with families, healthcare professionals, and local authorities to ensure seamless, coordinated care.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional, person-centred care that empowers individuals to live independently and with dignity in their own homes. We are committed to providing high-quality support services that enhance quality of life and promote well-being.

With our diverse team of qualified professionals including doctors, nurses, social workers, carers, and support workers, we ensure good quality services that prioritise your loved ones' needs. Our approach is holistic, considering not just physical needs but also emotional, social, and psychological well-being.

Through continuous improvement, staff development, and adherence to best practices, we strive to set the standard for excellence in home care services across PE7, Peterborough, Cambridgeshire and surrounding areas.

Why Choose Us

Choosing Apex Sanctuary means choosing a partner dedicated to your well-being. We stand out through our commitment to excellence, professional expertise, and unwavering dedication to our service users.
